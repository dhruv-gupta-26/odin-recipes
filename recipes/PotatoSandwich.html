Potato Sandwich

Description

This item is one of the easiest to make, yet it satiates the hunger as well as leaves a good taste on the tongue too.
Let's try to look at how it's made

Ingredients:

  • Bread (Brown/White)
  • Boiled Potatoes (mashed)
  • Onion (optional)
  • Tomatoes (optional)
  • Spices
  • Toaster
  • Sandwich spread (optional)

Steps:

  1. First mix the mashed potatoes and sprinkle them with spices
  2. Oil both sides of bread very lightly, and place the mixture between two breads
  3. You can also add sandwich spread if you have it with you, it'll give a distinct flavour to the dish
  4. Place the bread in toaster and let it heat until the surface of bread becomes golden brown
  5. Accompany the dish with sauce or ketchup of your choice!
