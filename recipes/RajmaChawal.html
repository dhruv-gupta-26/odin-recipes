Rajma Chawal

Description

This is one of the most loved dish in India. Bowl of Rice mixed with delectable curry of kidney beans, good for health and great in taste as well.

Below we'd like to display the recipe for this dish.

Items needed:

  • Rice
  • Kidney Beans
  • Water
  • Spices to add
  • Tomato, onion and garlic
  • Jeera

So let's look at the steps to make this wonderful of a meal

  1. Soak the kidney beans in water overnight and then rinse them
  2. Now take a glass full of rice and rinse it properly
  3. Now start boiling rice in water and add salt and jeera to it.
  4. Let the rice with water boil on high flame till the water almost dries out. And then lower the flame for better cooking and to avoid burning of rice
  5. Prepare the tadka with tomato, onion and garlic, and then add oil and spices to it.
  6. Let this tadka on stove, till it gets properly cooked.
  7. And then add water and kidney beands to it
  8. Put it in a cooker, and let it cook for 4-5 whistles
  9. Pour Jeera Rice and mix it with Rajma to enjoy the delectable
